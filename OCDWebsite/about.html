Hi I am a student and have a keen interest in knowing about psychological disorders and their treatments. I have always felt like India is lacking in the positive attitude and seriousness that is needed to cope and handle a mental disorder. This my contribution to my society in spreading awareness about OCD.


OCD is a psychiatric disorder that can be treated by taking medicines from doctors and a cognitive behaviour therapy which aids patient in improving clarity and maturity needed to handle this disorder. It is identified using a checklist of behaviours by qualified professionals. Family support is extremely important in coping up with this disorder. Patient needs to be informed about the signs, treatment, recovery and fluctuating nature of the disorder.
